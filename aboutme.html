Hello! I'm Madeline.

The process of creating art has always been a path to self-discovery. Creativity has always been at the center of who I am. While graphic design is my primary focus, I also enjoy exploring other artistic mediums, including jewelry making, ceramics, and photography. I love the process of turning ideas into something tangible, whether that means designing on a screen, shaping clay by hand, or capturing a moment through a camera lens.

A BIT MORE ABOUT ME…

Outside of my artistic work, I work as a childcare provider and seasonally teach figure skating to beginner skaters. Both experiences have strengthened my patience, communication skills, and appreciation for creativity and personal growth in others. I’m obsessed with Star Wars, action and sci-fi films and occasionally will find myself Infront of an TV playing sims 4. I began my college education at Connecticut State Community College – Tunxis Campus earning my Associate of Science degree in Graphic Design. I am currently preparing to continue my education by pursuing a bachelor’s degree in graphic design beginning in Fall 2026.
