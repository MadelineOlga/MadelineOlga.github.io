Hello! I'm Madeline.

My name is Madeline Griggs, and I am a graphic designer and artist with a passion for creativity in many forms. I earned my Associate of Science degree in Graphic Design from Connecticut State Community College – Tunxis Campus and am currently preparing to continue my education by pursuing a bachelor’s degree in graphic design beginning in Fall 2026. Creativity has always been at the center of who I am. While graphic design is my primary focus, I also enjoy exploring other artistic mediums, including jewelry making, ceramics, and photography. I love the process of turning ideas into something tangible, whether that means designing on a screen, shaping clay by hand, or capturing a moment through a camera lens. Outside of my artistic work, I work as a childcare provider and seasonally teach figure skating to beginner skaters. Both experiences have strengthened my patience, communication skills, and appreciation for creativity and personal growth in others. When I’m not creating, you can usually find me relaxing with my three cats and dog, working on a new project, or enjoying a great action movie.
